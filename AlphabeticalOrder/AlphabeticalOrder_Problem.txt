Title: 
Alphabetical Order

Problem: 
You are given a list of contac names.Order it alphabetically.

Input format: 
Sequence of contact names,each on a new line.
Names are non-empty and consist only of lowercase english letters.
Total lenght of names is no more than 10,000.

Output format:
Output given names in alphabetical order each name on a new line.

Sample input:
turing
dijkstra
knuth

Sample output:
dijkstra
knuth
turing
